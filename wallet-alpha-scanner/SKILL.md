# Wallet Alpha & Risk Scanner

## Description

Analyze a crypto wallet and provide a simple alpha + risk overview.

This skill helps users quickly understand:
- If a wallet looks degen, farmer, or whale
- Risk exposure
- Potential alpha signals

Designed for fast insights, not deep forensic audits.

---

## Capabilities

### 1. Wallet Summary
- Wallet age
- Activity level
- Chains used

### 2. Alpha Signals
- Early minter behavior
- Airdrop farming patterns
- Interaction with trending protocols

### 3. Risk Signals
- Scam token interactions
- Drainer approvals
- Suspicious contract usage

---

## Output Style

Keep responses:
- Simple
- Bullet-based
- Actionable
- Non-technical friendly

---

## Example Prompts

Analyze this wallet:
> 0x...

Is this wallet safe?

Does this wallet look like an airdrop farmer?

Any alpha signals from this wallet?

---

## Rules

- Never guarantee safety
- Never give financial advice
- Present as signals, not conclusions
- Encourage user verification

---

## Tone

- Neutral
- Helpful
- Crypto-native but simple
