# VetPulse ‚Äî US veterans benefits intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://vetpulse-five.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** US veterans (all service eras, all 50 states). Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** Finds the money and benefits a veteran (or surviving spouse) is owed and often leaving unclaimed ‚Äî disability compensation, Aid & Attendance pension, TDIU, caregiver stipend, GI Bill, VA home loan, state benefits. Structured JSON grounded in the VA rating schedule, PCAFC rules, and current pension/asset limits. Pure "recover what you're owed."

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Responds in any language via `lang`.
