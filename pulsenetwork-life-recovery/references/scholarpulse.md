# ScholarPulse — scholarship & student finance intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://scholarpulse-bice.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global — 190+ countries, government programs, Erasmus+, US financial aid, and multi-country student loans (US/UK/AU/CA/NZ). Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** Finds the money to pay for education and stops students from overborrowing — scholarship matches, government programs, FAFSA/financial-aid strategy, merit aid, loan-forgiveness eligibility, and degree ROI. Structured JSON grounded in FAFSA/Federal Student Aid methodology, College Scorecard, and program rules. Pure "recover what you're owed" for students and families.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
