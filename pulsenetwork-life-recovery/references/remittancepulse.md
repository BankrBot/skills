# RemittancePulse — remittance & cross-border money intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://remittancepulse.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global — 200+ corridors covering the $700B+ annual remittance market. Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** Stops migrants and diaspora families from overpaying to send money home — the *true* total cost of a transfer (fee + hidden FX markup) across providers, for any corridor, plus how to receive it and the compliance rules. Structured JSON. The recovered money is real: the difference between the cheapest and typical provider is often 5–10% of the amount sent.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
