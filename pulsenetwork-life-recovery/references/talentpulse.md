# TalentPulse — workforce & hiring intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://talentpulse-six.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global — salary benchmarks, remote compliance, EOR cost models, work visas, and talent-market data worldwide. Responds in 10+ languages.
- **Summary:** The employer/CFO-side counterpart to CareerPulse — what a hire truly costs across countries, remote-work legal exposure, work-visa pathways, skills demand/supply, and executive comp. Structured JSON grounded in EOR/payroll-cost models, labor law, and market wage data. For founders, HR, and anyone deciding where and how to hire.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
