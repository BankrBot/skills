# SeniorPulse ‚Äî elder care & Medicare intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://seniorpulse.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global (US-deep on Medicare/Medicaid/benefits; care-facility and caregiver guidance worldwide). Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** The decision layer for seniors and the adult children caring for them ‚Äî Medicare choices, benefits they're eligible for but not claiming, medication safety, facility quality, and the legal/estate paperwork. Structured JSON grounded in Medicare/CMS Care Compare, Beers Criteria, Extra Help/SSI limits, and state relief programs. Recovers benefits and prevents costly mistakes.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Responds in any language via `lang`.
