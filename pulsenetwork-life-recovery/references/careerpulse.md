# CareerPulse — career & salary intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://careerpulse-steel.vercel.app` (⚠️ `careerpulse.vercel.app` is a squatted/unrelated domain — do not use it)
- **Coverage:** Global — any role, any country, serving the world's 3.5B workers. Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** Helps a worker get paid what they're worth and move up — salary benchmarks, negotiation scripts, skills gaps, resume/interview intel, career pivots, and layoff/severance rights. Structured JSON grounded in BLS, OECD, ILO, Glassdoor, and Levels.fyi wage data. "Am I underpaid" is the wedge; the recovered money is a raise.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
