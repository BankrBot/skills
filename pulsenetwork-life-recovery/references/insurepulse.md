# InsurePulse — insurance intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://insurepulse.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global (US-deep on rate/coverage norms; jurisdiction-aware elsewhere)
- **Summary:** Tells a human whether they're over- or under-covered, what a policy should cost, where the coverage gaps are, and how to stop overpaying — the intelligence layer that precedes a quote or a denial appeal, not a quote engine. Structured JSON grounded in NAIC/state filing norms and actuarial rules of thumb.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. All params are optional free-text; add `lang` for any language.
