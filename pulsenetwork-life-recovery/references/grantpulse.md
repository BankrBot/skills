# GrantPulse — grant discovery & application intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://grantpulse-three.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global — US federal/state/foundation, EU (Horizon/Erasmus), and global development funding. Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** Finds the free money an org or individual qualifies for and helps win it — matched grants, eligibility checks, deadlines, and drafted application narratives. Structured JSON grounded in Grants.gov and USASpending.gov. "Recover what you're owed" for nonprofits, small businesses, researchers, and individuals.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
