# EduPulse — education & exam-prep intelligence

- **Base URL:** `https://edupulse-xi-blond.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global — any subject, K–PhD, 190+ countries, and 200+ standardized/professional exams. Responds in any language.
- **Summary:** The study engine — generate a study guide, drill practice questions matched to the real exam's rubric, diagnose exactly why an answer was wrong, and plan the whole prep timeline. Structured JSON, exam-rubric-matched. Helps a learner actually pass the licensing/entrance exam that unlocks a career.

Call any endpoint with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`. Inspect first with `bankr x402 schema "<baseUrl><path>"`. Add `lang` for any language.
