# Drop-Day War Room
Real-time monitoring and strategy for high-heat sneaker drops, including stock tracking, raffle aggregation, and shock-drop alerts.

## Capabilities
- **Real-Time Stock Tracking**: Monitors inventory levels at major Shopify boutiques (Kith, Undefeated, Concepts) and Nike SNKRS.
- **Raffle Aggregator**: Automatically collects and organizes active raffle links for upcoming high-heat releases.
- **Shock-Drop Alerts**: Scrapes social feeds and monitor channels for unannounced drops or restocks.
- **Post-Drop Sentiment**: Analyzes social hype and early sales data to predict immediate resale premiums and "hold vs. sell" strategies.

## Workflow
1. **Setup**: Input the style code (e.g., "DZ5485-612") for the upcoming drop.
2. **Monitor**: Initialize scrapers for Shopify stock endpoints and social monitors.
3. **Alert**: Push notifications for stock changes, raffle openings, or shock-drop signatures.
4. **Analyze**: Generate a "Heat Map" showing where stock is sitting longest and which sizes are moving fastest.

## Safety & Compliance
- **Rate Limiting**: Uses proxy rotation and rate limiting to avoid IP bans from major retailers.
- **Data Integrity**: Cross-references multiple stock monitors to ensure accuracy.
- **Privacy**: All monitored data and raffle entries are kept private to the user's session.
