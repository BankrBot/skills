# onchainpulse — onchain finance, RWA, tokenization & regulation

- **baseUrl:** `https://onchainpulse-nine.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global (US, EU/MiCA, UK, APAC; jurisdiction + `lang` params)
- **Payment:** x402, USDC on Base. All endpoints $0.10 unless noted. Call with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`.

Intelligence for the onchain financial transition: decode crypto legislation into plain English with sector impact, track real-world-asset (RWA) tokenization markets and institutional flows, model regulatory scenarios, and get practical onboarding/compliance guidance. Structured JSON, grounded in primary regulatory text and market data — better than a model guessing. **This vertical also hosts the two flagship pre-trade token scanners** (mirrored on Bankr x402 Cloud).
