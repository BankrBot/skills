# cryptopulse — DeFi yield, crypto security, threats, tax & onboarding

- **baseUrl:** `https://cryptopulse-xi-five.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global (all endpoints take a `country` param; DeFi across Ethereum/Base/Arbitrum/Berachain/Solana)
- **Payment:** x402, USDC on Base. All endpoints $0.10. Call with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`.

Global crypto intelligence: live DeFi yield/APY (DeFiLlama TVL + rates), personalized allocation strategy, custody/security frameworks, active threat intel, and country-specific tax/exchange/onboarding/spending/banking/merchant guidance. Structured JSON from real sources (DeFiLlama, GoPlus, curated hack/threat DB) — better than a model guessing.
