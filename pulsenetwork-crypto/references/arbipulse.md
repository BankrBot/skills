# arbipulse — cross-market arbitrage scanning & execution

- **baseUrl:** `https://arbipulse.vercel.app`
- **Coverage:** Global (multi-chain DeFi, global CEXs, international sportsbooks, global commodity hubs)
- **Payment:** x402, USDC on Base. All endpoints $0.10. Call with `bankr x402 call "<baseUrl><path>?<params>"`.

Scans live arbitrage opportunities across DeFi yield spreads, DEX/CEX price differentials, perpetual funding rates, sports surebets, ETF/NAV gaps, commodity regional pricing and statistical pairs — then sizes and packages execution. Structured JSON from real sources (DeFiLlama, exchange APIs, Hyperliquid funding, sportsbook odds feeds) — better than a model guessing. Numbers are net of fees/gas/slippage where the endpoint models them.
