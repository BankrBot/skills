# Multimodal — AI Image, Meme & NFT Generation

**Skill name:** `multimodal`

Generate stunning visuals and mint them as NFTs. Perfect for token launches, PFPs, memes, marketing, and agent creativity.

## Capabilities

- Text-to-Image (Grok Imagine, Flux, etc.)
- Meme generation with templates
- Short video / GIF / animation support
- Batch generation + variations
- Automatic IPFS upload + NFT minting
- Full style, aspect ratio, and quality control

## Natural Language Triggers

- "Generate a cyberpunk PFP for $MYTOKEN"
- "Create 3 funny memes about Base memecoins and mint the best one"
- "Make a professional banner for my project"
- "Generate a short animated intro video"
- "Create 5 variations of a logo"
